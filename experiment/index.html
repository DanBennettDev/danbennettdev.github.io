HELP ME OUT!

Please take part in my current experiment! Recruiting participants during the pandemic is hard and I need to get this data to complete my PhD.

It takes up to 70 mins in total (over two sessions) and you can receive up to £40 in Amazon vouchers for taking part! You can also learn some interesting things about cognitive science, and the design of new technologies to support productivity. You can pick your own time slots to suit you.

Overview: This a fun experiment. I’ll meet you over zoom for each session (you needn’t to show your face - only share your screen for part of the session). You’ll do some writing, based on interesting memories, and also a brain-training game. In previous experiments people particularly enjoyed the writing bit.

Afterwards I’ll be happy to tell you more if you’re interested. I’m also happy to chat to MSc students about their upcoming projects, particularly in HCI or data.

Money: Everybody who completes the experiment will receive £10. On top of this there are two additional £30 voucher prizes. One is a prize draw for all participants. The other is a prize for performance performer. Since we expect about 30 participants you have a decent chance winning one of these extra prizes.

Requirements: You just need to be fluent in English. By “fluent” I mean either English should be your first language or you should have 5+ years of experience writing regularly in English - emails, essays, etc. You also need to avoid drinking caffeine for 3 hours before my experiment.
